What we solve

Finance operations challenges we help you solve.

Five recurring problems we hear from CFOs, audit partners and PE finance leads — and the way Windy Street resolves each one.

  1. 01

    Stretched bandwidth during peak cycles

    Month-end close, year-end reporting and audits all hit at the same time. We add senior capacity exactly when you need it — and step back when you don't — without permanent headcount cost.

  2. 02

    Inconsistent output quality

    Junior offshore work that needs partner review every time defeats the purpose of outsourcing. Our specialists deliver Big-4 grade output the first time, with senior review built into every engagement.

  3. 03

    Hiring & retention of specialist talent

    US-CPA, CBV, CFA, EA — this talent is hard to find and harder to keep. Our pre-vetted bench gives you immediate access to specialists across accounting, audit, tax, valuations and FDD.

  4. 04

    Lack of visibility & control

    Black-box delivery models hide problems until it's too late. You get live dashboards, a named engagement partner, audit-ready documentation and direct access to the team doing the work.

  5. 05

    Scaling without compromise

    Growth means more entities, more tickets, more complexity. Our flexible commercial models — FTE, project, retainer, hybrid — scale up or down without friction or front-loaded cost.
